Integral University

Integral University: An Overview Integral University is a prominent private university located in Lucknow, the capital city of Uttar Pradesh, India. Established in 1998, the university has grown to become one of the leading educational institutions in the region. It is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and offers a wide array of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines, including engineering, management, law, science, pharmacy, and healthcare. With a vision to provide comprehensive education, Integral University aims to cultivate skilled professionals who can contribute to the development of society. Academic Programs Integral University offers diverse academic programs tailored to meet the needs of various fields. The university’s curriculum focuses on both theoretical knowledge and practical learning, ensuring that students are well-prepared for the challenges of the real world. Engineering and Technology: Integral University is particularly well-known for its engineering programs. The university offers undergraduate programs (B.Tech) in several branches, including Computer Science, Mechanical Engineering, Civil Engineering, Electrical Engineering, and Electronics Engineering. Additionally, postgraduate programs (M.Tech) in specialized areas like Data Science, Artificial Intelligence, and VLSI Design are also available. The engineering curriculum emphasizes innovation, research, and practical exposure through labs, projects, and internships.
